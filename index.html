How to Calculate Your College GPA

GPA (Grade Point Average) is a standard way to measure academic performance in college. Each course grade is converted into points on a 4.0 scale and weighted by credit hours.

To calculate your GPA, multiply each course grade by its credit value, add all results, and divide by the total number of credits. This calculator does everything automatically for you.

GPA Scale (4.0)

  • A = 4.0
  • B = 3.0
  • C = 2.0
  • D = 1.0
  • F = 0.0
