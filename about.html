About This Website

This website was created to help students easily calculate their Grade Point Average (GPA) using commonly accepted grading scales.

Our goal is to provide a simple, fast, and accessible educational tool that can be used by students around the world. The calculator is designed for informational purposes only and should not replace official academic records or institutional evaluations.

This website does not require user registration and does not collect personal information directly from visitors.

If you have questions or suggestions, feel free to reach out through the platform where this website is hosted.
